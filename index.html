⚡ Bulk vs Run: How to Fuel Both
Running and bulking seem contradictory — running burns calories, bulking requires a surplus. The solution: eat more on run days, slightly less on rest days.
Tue/Thu/Sat run days: Add 1 extra scoop whey or 200g extra rice to M2 or M4.
Sunday full rest: Drop M5 snack entirely.
Protein (293g) is the most critical number — never miss it regardless of other calorie adjustments.
