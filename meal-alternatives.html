Swapping Rules — Read Before You Rotate

  • Never swap M1 + M2 carbs for low-GI options. Pre and post-workout carbs MUST be fast-digesting — banana, dates, white rice, white bread. This is the one rule with zero flexibility.
  • Keep protein sources rotating. Each protein source has a different amino acid profile. Chicken, fish, eggs, paneer, dal — use all of them through the week for better muscle building.
  • When using "adjust slightly" swaps — just compensate in the same meal. E.g., if paneer adds more fat, remove ghee from rice. You don't need to recalculate everything.
  • Veg days (Thu/Sun rest days) are perfect for vegetarian meals — paneer, dal, rajma, chana, tofu. Lower activity = you don't need animal protein every meal.
  • Fish 2–3x per week is the single best upgrade you can make to this plan. Natural omega-3 reduces DOMS, improves sleep, and supports muscle protein synthesis better than any supplement except creatine.
  • Sunday meal prep: Batch cook chicken/fish for Mon–Wed. Boil eggs. Prep rice. This takes 1.5 hours and makes all swaps easy to execute at office without thinking.
