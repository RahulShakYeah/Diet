Race Morning Checklist
  • 01
    Wake 2.5 hrs before start. Eat familiar carbs — oats, toast, banana. Nothing new on race day. Ever.
  • 02
    Hydrate smart. Sip 400–500 ml water + electrolytes over 2 hours. Don't gulp.
  • 03
    Warm up properly. Arrive 40 min early. 8 min easy jog + 3×100m strides at race pace.
  • 04
    Wear what you trained in. Shoes, socks, shorts — all familiar. Not the day for new gear.
  • 05
    Gel at km 10. Only if practiced in training. Take at every aid station — slow 3 sec to drink properly.
  • 06
    160–170 BPM playlist. This tempo naturally lifts your cadence and matches ideal stride rate.
  • 07
    Carb load Thu + Fri. Pasta, rice, bread. No new foods. No spicy, no alcohol.
  • 08
    Sleep Friday night matters most. In bed by 9:30 PM. Saturday complete rest.
Sub 1:45 is not just possible — with this plan it's the expected outcome.
Trust the Saturday long runs. Don't skip Tuesdays. Eat your 293g protein every day.
