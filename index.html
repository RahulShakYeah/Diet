⚡ The Plan

Strategy Overview

Lean bulk while training for a half marathon — body recomposition at its peak.

The Strategy: Lean Bulk While Training for a Half Marathon

At 5'10", 69 kg, 28 years old — your TDEE is ~2,900–3,100 kcal. To gain lean mass while running high mileage, you need ~3,700–3,800 kcal/day with 290g+ protein. The running burns fat while the 3,740 cal surplus builds muscle. Expect ~0.4–0.5 kg lean mass per week. The PPL split (Sun/Tue/Thu) is structured around your running days (Mon/Wed/Fri) so you never lift heavy the day before a key run.

Outdoor vs Treadmill — How to Convert

Set treadmill incline to 1% for easy runs, 1.5% for tempo, 2% for race pace intervals. Speed settings: 4:58/km = 12.1 km/h · 5:10/km = 11.6 km/h · 5:30/km = 10.9 km/h · 6:00/km = 10.0 km/h · 6:15/km = 9.6 km/h · 6:30/km = 9.2 km/h

🥗 Fuel

Bulk Nutrition Plan

3,740 kcal · 6 Meals · 293g Protein. At 5'10", 69 kg, training 6 days/week — eat ~700 kcal above TDEE.

Bulk vs Run: How to Fuel Both

On Mon/Wed/Fri (run days), add 1 extra scoop of whey or 200g extra rice to M2 or M4. On Saturday (active recovery), drop M5 snack. This keeps your weekly average at ~3,700 kcal while optimally fuelling each session. Protein (293g) is the most important number — never miss it regardless of calorie adjustments.

🏁 Race Day

May 23, 2026 — Sub 1:45

Target pace: 4:58/km · Start conservative · Negative split · Empty the tank at km 19

The Golden Rule: First 5 km Must Feel Easy

Sub 1:45 is 4:58/km average. Go out at 5:05–5:10/km for the first 5 km. It will feel almost too slow. That's correct. Your race begins at km 10. Negative split = run the second half faster than the first half.

Sip 400–500 ml water with electrolytes over 2 hours. Don't gulp all at once.

Arrive 40 min early. 8 min easy jog + 3×100m strides at race pace.

Wear what you trained in
Shoes, socks, shorts — all familiar. Race day is NOT the day for new gear.

Gel at km 10
Only if practiced in a long run. Take at every aid station — slow 3 sec to drink properly.
